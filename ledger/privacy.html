Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-28

Overview

Sloth's Ledger is an independently developed mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

  • Your financial data is stored locally on your device.
  • No financial data is transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information.

Information handled by the app

Sloth's Ledger allows you to enter financial information such as transactions, categories, accounts, balances, budgets, subscriptions, and holdings. This information is stored locally on your device.

Data collection

The app does not collect personal data on external servers. All financial entries remain on your device unless you choose to export them.

Analytics and crash reporting

The app does not include third-party analytics or tracking by default.

Permissions

The app may request device permissions when required for a specific feature (for example, file access for import or export). Permissions are optional and can be managed in your device settings.

Data sharing

Because data is not collected or stored on external servers, we do not share personal financial information with third parties.

Data retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it within the app, uninstall the app, or use an in-app reset or delete feature.

Children

Sloth's Ledger is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and is intended for general audiences.

Security

Reasonable security practices are followed in development. However, no method of storage is completely secure. You are responsible for securing access to your device.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the latest revision.

Contact

Sloth's Ledger is developed independently. For privacy-related questions, contact: slothmock1@proton.me
