Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-28

Summary

  • This app stores your financial entries locally on your device.
  • We do not sell your data.

What data the app handles

Sloth Ledger lets you enter financial information such as transactions, categories, accounts, balances, budgets, subscriptions, and holdings. This information is stored locally on your device.

Data collection

We do not collect personal data on our servers through the app.

Analytics and crash reporting

By default, the app does not include third-party analytics.

Permissions

The app may request device permissions only when needed for a feature (for example, file access for import/export). Permissions are optional and you can manage them in your device settings.

Data sharing

We do not share your data with third parties as it is not collected.

Data retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it within the app, uninstall the app, or use an in-app reset/delete feature.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and is intended for general audiences.

Security

We aim to follow reasonable security practices, but no method of storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your device.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the effective date and publish the new version on this page.
