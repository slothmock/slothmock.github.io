Caves Of Crisis - Roguelike Game

Caves of Crisis is a roguelike adventure where players navigate procedurally generated caves, balancing survival stats and engaging in creative item interactions through the "Think it, do it" system. With dynamic status effects, strategic combat, and real-time progression, each descent offers a challenging and unpredictable journey.
